Delete Your Account & Data

Developer: Benzi Heler · App: SpeakIt

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your SpeakIt account and all associated data, send an email to the address below. We will process your request within 30 days.

benziheler@gmail.com

In your email, please include:

  1. The email address or account identifier you used to sign in (Google or Facebook).
  2. A statement confirming that you want your account and data permanently deleted.

What Data Is Deleted

When you request deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your Firebase authentication account (email, provider ID).
  • Your leaderboard entries (name and score).
  • Your saved game progress associated with your account.
  • Any duel room history linked to your account.

What Data Is Kept

The following data is retained, in anonymized form, for up to 90 days after deletion for service integrity and analytics:

  • Aggregated, anonymous Firebase Analytics event counts (no personal identifiers).
  • Server-side logs that may contain your IP address, kept only for security and abuse prevention.

After 90 days, all anonymized records are automatically purged.

Partial Data Deletion

If you want to delete only specific data (for example, just your leaderboard entry) without deleting your entire account, mention that in your email.

Questions

If you have any questions about this process, contact us at benziheler@gmail.com.
