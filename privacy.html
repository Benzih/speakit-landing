Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

SpeakIt ("the App") is a voice-powered guessing game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

The App uses your device's microphone solely for speech recognition during gameplay. Voice data is processed by Google's Speech-to-Text service on your device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

We collect:

  • Game progress - stored locally on your device (levels completed, scores, settings).
  • Firebase Analytics - anonymous usage data to improve the App (no personally identifiable information).

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information.
  • We do not record or store your voice.
  • We do not share any data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Microphone Permission

The App requires microphone access to enable voice-based gameplay. The microphone is only active during gameplay rounds when the speech recognition indicator is visible. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Speech-to-Text - processes voice input for gameplay.
  • Google Firebase - provides game data and anonymous analytics.

These services have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal data to play.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
